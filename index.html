Our Motive

To promote science with humanity and transform the society through innovative education / learning methods

Mission

  • To create and propagate the science learning temperament amongst children in rural, semi-urban, and urban areas, particularly to underprivileged.
  • Develop human beings as capital through training and skill development and build ideal/role model villages.

Vision

To build a better society through principles of Humanity with science, eradicate blind faith/beliefs (using science), develop villages and citizens (as role models) by providing opportunities and training for inclusive growth, education for all.
