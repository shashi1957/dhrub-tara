Our Objectives

The principle is to supplement the formal education system and develop ecosystem for building progressive society and nation.

EDUCATION

  • To create interest in learning science amongst children of rural areas.
  • To develop scientific temperament amongst primary and secondary students.
  • To promote science with humanity.
  • Develop human resources of nation through training / education.
  • To promote / execute the various educational schemes of government.
  • To plan and execute training of trainers (teachers).
  • To promote academies.
  • To provide financial / academic support to students.
  • To create educational institutions.

SOCIAL AND ECONOMICAL DEVELOPMENT

  • To spread the scientific knowledge in society.
  • To eradicate blind faith from society through scientific awareness.
  • To develop skill through scientific training.
  • Provide legal aid to needy / poor people.
  • To awaken young minds about patriotism and national consciousness.
  • To promote and develop sports and allied activities for making a healthy society.
  • To develop sports / educational infrastructure in rural areas.
  • To provide financial assistance to economically weaker sections.

DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION

  • To undertake implementation of digital technology and systems in the field of social science and medical equipment.
  • To provide computer literacy.

HEALTH CARE

  • To undertake data collection for health monitoring system.
  • To undertake research programs on data analysis for development of health monitoring system.
  • To implement health schemes of government and private sectors.

AGRICULTURE

  • To bring awareness about advancements in agriculture.
  • To expose the farmers to agricultural technologies.
  • To facilitate government schemes regarding agriculture.
  • To bring new technology for improvement in yield and crop.
  • Application of drones in the agricultural field.
